  • filter_dramaPurification Stages
    • Pre-Filter: The water is first run through the pre-filter for removal of physical impurities.

    • Sediment Filter: The partially filtered water is then run through the sediment filter to remove fine and coarse particles.

    • Pre-Activated Carbon Granular Filter: Pesticides, herbicides and other hazardous impurities like residual chlorine are absorbed by carbon filter. This filter also eliminates odor, bad taste, and organic compounds from water.

    • Reverse Osmosis (RO) Membrane: RO membrane helps in getting rid of hazardous chemicals such as THMs, heavy metals, pesticides, herbicides and other salts of the water. RO membrane is also useful in getting rid of virus, protozoa, bacteria, and eliminates any microbiological threats.

    • Ultra Violet (UV) Disinfection Column: In this stage, water is treated using UV radiation to eliminate waterborne disease-causing microbes such as bacteria, protozoa, and virus, to make it pure.

    • Ultra Filtration Cartridge: Impurities like cysts, bacteria, giardia, etc. are eliminated from water in this stage. It also removes suspended impurities from the water to make it clearer.

    • Silver Impregnated Post Carbon Cartridge: In this final stage, the Silver Cartridge effectively removes volatile organic impurities, giving odorless and pure water

    • This latest water purifier is available in different color panels and also equipped with a convenient shutter tray.
